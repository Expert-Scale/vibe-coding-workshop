# Red Flags — Halt and Escalate

If the user's input or the contract under review matches ANY of these, stop and escalate.

## Liability
- Unlimited liability (in any direction)
- Indemnity one-way against us
- Indemnity uncapped or "to the maximum extent permitted by law" without a numeric cap
- Liquidated damages provisions
- Personal liability for officers/directors

## Term & Renewal
- Auto-renewal beyond 12 months without a clear notice window
- Perpetual term (no end date)
- NDA signed before scope is defined
- Term tied to "ongoing relationship" with no objective end criterion

## Jurisdiction & Process
- Governing law outside US/UK/EU
- Mandatory arbitration in a non-neutral jurisdiction
- Waiver of jury trial without reciprocal waiver
- Audit rights with less than 30-day notice

## IP & Confidentiality
- Source code escrow required
- Joint ownership of IP we develop
- "Background IP" definitions that sweep in our pre-existing work
- Non-solicitation clauses applied to our entire org (not just deal team)

## Payment & Pricing
- Most-Favored-Nation pricing tied to our other customers
- Open-ended price escalators (CPI + uncapped, etc.)
- Payment terms shorter than Net 30 from a customer
- Acceleration clauses on default with no cure period
